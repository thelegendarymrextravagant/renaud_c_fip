The Grim Reality of Burnout

Burnout often leaves people feeling extremely tired.

Some days, you might not want to get out of bed at all.

More often than not, the hardest part of someone's day is the first step.

Stress often keeps me awake at night, leading to me sleeping well into the morning.

Even with five alarms set, I find it difficult to roll out of bed at a "decent" time.

Some days are better than others, but every day is a battle in and of itself.

How Stress Eats Away At You

Stress and burnout often lead people to indulge in unhealthy coping mechanisms such as drinking. Other noticable warning signs include frequently cancelling plans or an overwhelming desire to do nothing.
